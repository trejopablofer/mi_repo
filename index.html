Two screens. Double the fun.

Having two 256x192 displays gives you a more immersive experience! Keep the action going on one screen while you look at you character's stats on the other, among many other creative uses depending on the game!

Touching is good.

Poke, draw and slide your Stylus across the bottom screen! This new touch input offers you a new way to play your favourite Nintendo DS games!

You're playing with power.

Enjoy fast-paced frantic 2D games as well as immersive polygonal 3D games! With its two 32-bit processors the Nintendo DS can render action on both screens while playing high-quality sound! This little bad boy quite packs a punch!

Advanced. Literally.

Don't leave your Game Boy Advance cartridges locked inside your drawer! Relive your favorite GBA titles thanks to the GBA cartridge slot on your Nintendo DS! (local multiplayer on GBA titles not supported).
