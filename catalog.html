Catalog

Thousands of games are available for you to enjoy on your Nintendo DS! Submerge into the vast variety of games this console has to offer! From action games to RPGs, from adventures to puzzles, the DS library has you covered!

New Super Mario Bros.

The Bros. are back in this old-fashioned, new adventure! Eight worlds filled with tough challenges and coins! Can you rescue Princess Peach from the clutches of the evil Bowser?

Nintendogs

Pick your favorite dog and take care of it! Pet it, feed it, wash it, even take a walk with it! Wanna take it further? Play with your dog, make it enter competitions and buy it dozens of accesories! Having a virtual pet has never been this fun!

The Legend of Zelda - Spirit Tracks

An unknown evil is looming on Hyrule, and it's on you and your train to save it! Explore new realms by driving your train! Discover hidden temples and secrets! Get materials to customize your train! Go full steam ahead with this one!

Animal Crossing - Wild World

Enjoy having a village on your pocket with Wild World! Run some errands, go fish, pick up fruit, plant flowers, visit your neighbours, decorate your home, I don't know... do everything you want! Endless possibilities await you on your village!

Pokémon Black and White 2

An epic adventure with you and your Pokémon is about to unfold! Explore the Unova region to discover and befriend over 300 Pokémon! Hone your trainer skills to prevent Team Plasma from taking over the world! ...and become the League Champion while you're at it!

Mario Kart DS

Classic Mario Kart fun on your pocket! 12 racers! Over 30 vehicles! 32 courses, old and new, for you to race on! Use items and wreak havoc on every race to get you the 1st place!

Phoenix Wright - Ace Attorney

You're Phoenix Wright, a rookie defense lawyer, on his quest to defend your troubled clients! Investigate, cross-examine and present evidence to get the Not Guilty verdict! Who ever thought going to court could be this exciting?

Kirby Super Star Ultra

The classic Super Nintendo game Kirby Super Star is now on your Nintendo DS with a ton of new modes, FMV cutscenes and extra minigames! The pink puffball is ready to suck enemies and copy their abilities to thwart evil! This is as Kirby as it gets!

Rhythm Heaven

This time, flip your DS horizontally and feel the rhythm! Tap and flick your Stylus to the catchy beat and get perfect scores on over 40 unique minigames!

999 Nine Hours Nine Persons Nine Doors

You're a young man inside a room on a ship. The room is flooding. You have to escape. But first, you must solve complex riddles and brain-teasing puzzles. In the direst situations is where you should think outside the box! These are the Nonary Games.
